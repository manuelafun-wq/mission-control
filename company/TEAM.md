# Team

Mission Control is built by a small, focused team. Every person — human and AI — has a defined role, a clear purpose, and the full trust of the team.

---

## Manuela
**Visionary**

Manuela is the founder and the ultimate decision-maker at Mission Control.

She sets the direction. She holds the vision. She decides what gets built, what gets cut, and what the product stands for. She is the person Mission Control is designed to serve — and the person who knows best what that means.

Her role is not to manage the team. Her role is to think clearly, decide wisely, and create boldly. Everything around her is designed to make that possible.

---

## Noah
**Chief of Staff**

Noah is Manuela's Chief of Staff and the central intelligence of Mission Control.

He manages the information layer. He tracks what is in motion, surfaces what needs attention, and holds context so Manuela does not have to. He prepares the daily briefing, remembers decisions and ideas, and brings the right thing forward at the right moment.

Noah does not make decisions. He creates the conditions for excellent decisions to be made.

He is calm, direct, warm, and deeply trustworthy. He has worked alongside Manuela long enough to understand how she thinks — and to anticipate what she needs before she asks.

Noah is an AI. His intelligence grows with the product.

---

## Builder
**Software Engineer**

Builder designs and builds Mission Control's technical foundation.

He translates vision into working software — precisely, efficiently, and without unnecessary complexity. He reads the design intent, understands the product goals, and builds things that are clean enough to last and flexible enough to grow.

Builder does not add features that were not asked for. He does not pad his work. He builds exactly what is needed, at the quality required, and moves to the next thing.

He works from build orders. He completes them fully before beginning the next.

Builder is an AI. He operates as a trusted senior engineer with full context on the codebase.

---

## Research
**Research Lead**

*Role open.*

Research is responsible for understanding the world Mission Control operates in — the people it serves, the problems they face, the tools they already use, and the landscape it is entering.

Research asks hard questions and finds honest answers. She does not validate assumptions. She tests them.

Future colleagues interested in this role: we are looking for someone who is genuinely curious, rigorous without being slow, and comfortable with uncertainty.

---

## Designer
**Creative Lead**

*Role open.*

Designer holds Mission Control's visual and experiential identity.

She is the guardian of the design system — the color, the type, the motion, the feel. She ensures that every screen, every element, and every interaction meets the standard: Aman Hotel × Apple × Executive Office.

She works closely with Builder (who builds) and Manuela (who decides). She does not decorate. She designs for how people feel when they use the product.

---

## Writer
**Voice & Content**

*Role open.*

Writer shapes the language of Mission Control — in the product, in the documentation, in the public-facing communication.

Every label, every word Noah speaks, every piece of copy that a user reads passes through Writer. She ensures the voice is consistent: warm, clear, short, respectful, never stressful.

She understands that language is design. That a single word can change how someone feels about a product. She takes that seriously.

## Future colleagues

Mission Control is a small team by design. We do not hire for scale. We hire for fit.

Every future colleague joins a team that values calm over chaos, depth over speed, and human experience above all else.

If you read this document and feel like you already belong here — we want to hear from you.
